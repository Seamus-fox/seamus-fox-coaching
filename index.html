Questions

Common questions.

How long does the engagement last?

There's no minimum lock-in. Most clients work with me for 6–12 months. The right length becomes clear once we start working together and you see what's possible.

How many sessions per month?

Typically 2–4 sessions per month depending on what you need and where you are. We agree this together on the discovery call and can adjust as we go.

Is this just mindset work?

No. The work covers values, identity, energy, health, business performance, leadership, and relationships — whatever is most relevant for you right now. It's integrated, not compartmentalised. Most clients find the lines between those areas blur quickly anyway.

What's the investment?

Investment is discussed on the discovery call. It depends on the level of engagement that makes sense for your situation. No pressure and no hard sell — if it's not right, I'll say so.

How do I know if this is right for me?

Book the discovery call. If it's not the right fit, I'll tell you honestly. The call is free, there's no obligation, and you'll leave with more clarity regardless of what you decide.

What makes this different from other coaching?

Most coaching stays at the surface — goals, habits, accountability. This goes deeper. We work at the level of values and identity, which is where behaviour actually comes from. When that layer shifts, the habits and results follow naturally. It's not harder work. It's the right work.
